How Sowo works

Find skill. Build trust.
Get it done.

Sowo replaces anonymous stars with real people vouching for real work. Because trust should come from someone you know.

01
Search by skill or location

Browse skilled providers near you by craft — hair, electrical, plumbing, childcare, carpentry, and more. Filter by availability, price, and your community.

02
Check their vouches

See who vouched for them — named people from your network, not strangers. Every vouch is a written endorsement from someone who knows their work.

03
Book, pay safely, vouch back

Book directly through the app. Payment is held in escrow and only released when you confirm the job is done. Then pass it forward — vouch for them.

Real skill. Real trust.
Sowo uses no fake urgency, no inflated ratings, and no paid promotions. Providers rise through the quality of their work and the strength of their community vouches — nothing else.
