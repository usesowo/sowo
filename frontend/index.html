How Sowo works

Find skill. Build trust.
Get it done.

Sowo replaces anonymous star ratings with real people vouching for real work — because trust should come from someone you know.

01
Search by skill or location

Browse skilled providers near you by craft — hair, electrical, plumbing, childcare, carpentry, and more. Filter by availability, price, and proximity.

01
02
Check their vouches

See who vouched for them — named people from your network, not strangers. Every vouch is a personal endorsement from someone who has seen their work firsthand.

02
03
Book, pay safely, vouch back

Book directly through the app. Payment is held in escrow and only released when you confirm the job is done well. Then pass it forward — vouch for them.

03
Real skill. Real trust.
Sowo uses no fake urgency, no inflated ratings, and no paid promotions. Providers rise through the quality of their work and the strength of their community vouches — nothing else.
