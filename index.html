Built differently, on purpose

No hidden fees, no anonymous reviews, no algorithmic gatekeeping. Just real people, recommending real people.

Named vouches, not stars

Every recommendation comes from a real person with a name and a face. No anonymous five-star theatre, no gamed ratings. If someone vouches, they put their name on it.

Talk first, book later

Message providers directly before you commit. Ask the questions that matter — about your hair, your skin, your space — and decide on your terms. No middleman.

Free to post, free to list

No card needed to post a request. No subscription to list your service. We only earn when you actually book — and even then, never from anonymous middlemen.
